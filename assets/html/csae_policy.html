Community Standards and Enforcement (CSAE)

At FindLove, we are committed to fostering a respectful and secure community. Our Community Standards are designed to ensure that all users enjoy a positive experience, free from harassment, discrimination, or abuse. These standards apply to all interactions and content shared on the platform.

Our Core Community Standards

  • Respect and Inclusion: Treat all users with kindness and respect, regardless of their background, beliefs, or preferences.
  • Consent and Boundaries: Always communicate with consent and respect others' boundaries. Unsolicited messages or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
  • No Hate or Violence: Content promoting hate speech, violence, racism, or discrimination will not be tolerated and may result in immediate account suspension.
  • Authenticity: Use your real identity and avoid creating fake or misleading profiles.
  • Safety First: Do not attempt to collect personal information such as financial data, passwords, or government IDs from other users.

Enforcement

We use both automated tools and manual moderation to detect violations of our standards. When a violation is identified, the following actions may be taken based on the severity:

  • Warning notice sent to the user.
  • Temporary or permanent suspension of the user's account.
  • Reporting to legal authorities if applicable (e.g., in cases of abuse or threats).

If you witness or experience any behavior that violates our standards, please report it immediately using the in-app reporting tools or contact our support team at contact@sorravlstudios.in. Your safety is our top priority.
